Meet the Founder of Cave Agency

Jess Godwin, MBA

As the founder, Jess brings forth a legacy of excellence and a profound commitment to helping businesses thrive. Hailing from the heart of Fort Worth, Texas, Jess pursued an education that would lay the foundation for a remarkable career in marketing. Graduating with a bachelor’s degree in Marketing from Tarleton State University, Jess honed her skills and deepened her understanding of the industry. Building upon this strong foundation, she pursued a Master of Business Administration from the same esteemed institution, solidifying her footprint as a proud Tarleton alumna.

Fuelled by a passion for assisting small businesses, Jess has dedicated her career to empowering entrepreneurs and maximizing profits for the betterment of both businesses and their communities. With a keen focus on marketing and operations, Jess possesses a wealth of experience and expertise in driving impactful campaigns that foster lasting connections and positive change.

A true Texan at heart, Jess finds joy in collaborating with business owners to bring their visions to life. Her strategic insights and innovative approach have helped numerous businesses achieve unparalleled success in the competitive market landscape.

Beyond her professional endeavors, Jess is known for her unwavering love for animals and her servant heart as a follower of Jesus. These values impact every aspect of her work, fostering genuine connections and driving meaningful change within the communities she collaborates with.

At Cave Agency, Jess guides businesses towards newfound heights of success. With a profound commitment to excellence and an unwavering dedication to her clients, Jess continues to redefine the landscape of marketing and business strategy as she empowers brands and drives change.
